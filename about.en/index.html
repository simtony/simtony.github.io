About this blog

My name is Tony Ou. I am a graduate in Computer Science of SusTech and now I work for Zhuiyi Techonology Inc., where I design machine learning algorithms to improve our customer service bot.

I found it necessary to write since it helps me clarify my thoughts. Lessons learnt and new ideas may dissipate if we don’t catch them, so I will write them down to review and share.

I’m still a active self-learner. I’m intrigue by the beautiful human intelligence, feel excited to learn more about it and share a passion for reverse engineering it with the AI industry. I will share ideas and thoughts as my understanding progress.

I may write in Chinese or English depending on the topic. I will try my best to make every post informative, and to achieve that goal some posts may be updated several times after first presented.

Hope you’ll learn something here :)
